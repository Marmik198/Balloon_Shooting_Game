INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY GAME :

   1. The game has three levels.
   2. The bow can be moved UP and DOWN with the help of arrow keys.
   3. The arrow can be shot using the right arrow key.
   4. Every time you shoot the balloon, you score 20 points.
   5. First level has 7 arrows and 12 balloons.
   6. You require to score 100 points to enter the second level.
   7. Second level has 7 arrows and 9 balloons.
   8. You require to score 200 points to enter the third level.
   9. Third level has 7 arrows and 7 balloons.
